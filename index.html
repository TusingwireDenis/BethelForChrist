About Our Church

Bethel For Christ Ministries International is a vibrant community of believers committed to spreading the Gospel and making disciples.

Our Vision & Mission

Founded in 2013 by Rev. Byamugisha Richard, Bethel For Christ Ministries International has grown from a small prayer group to a thriving church impacting our community and beyond.

Our vision is to heal the diseases of the soul, walk in His power, and transform their world through the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our mision is to Preach the Gospel and haverst human being souls to take them to Jesus
